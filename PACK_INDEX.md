# PACK_INDEX

## Skills
- **research-question-refiner**: Refine broad topics into focused, researchable questions with clear scope and variables.
- **search-string-designer**: Design search strings and source strategies for efficient, reproducible literature searches.
- **paper-screening-checker**: Screen candidate sources against relevance, quality, and scope criteria.
- **literature-theme-extractor**: Cluster literature into themes, schools of thought, methods, and trends.
- **gap-finder**: Identify unresolved questions, weak evidence areas, contradictions, and underexplored spaces.
- **methodology-comparer**: Compare study designs, methods, datasets, and evaluation choices across sources.
- **evidence-strength-scorer**: Assess the weight and credibility of evidence behind claims in the literature.
- **critical-analysis-writer**: Write critical discussion that distinguishes claims, evidence, assumptions, and limitations.
- **related-work-synthesizer**: Synthesize related work into a coherent narrative instead of a paper-by-paper list.
- **future-work-generator**: Generate grounded future work directions based on gaps, limitations, and practical constraints.
